Bajaj Pulsar 125 Summary

Price

Bajaj Pulsar 125 price for its variant - Pulsar 125 Disc starts at Rs. 1,08,681. The price for the other variants - Pulsar 125 Disc - Split Seat, Pulsar 125 Carbon Single Seat, Pulsar 125 Carbon Single Seat - Bluetooth, Pulsar 125 Carbon Split Seat and Pulsar 125 Carbon Split Seat - Bluetooth are Rs. 1,11,123, Rs. 1,16,625, Rs. 1,16,824, Rs. 1,18,866 and Rs. 1,22,074. The mentioned Pulsar 125 prices are the on-road price of Chennai.

About

The Pulsar 125 retains all the ethos that Bajaj’s iconic Pulsar family is known for but in a more accessible package. While the design is identical to that of the bigger Pulsar 150, the engine is a smaller unit with matching cycle parts.

Bajaj Pulsar 125 Overview

Design

The Bajaj Pulsar 125 features a muscular design similar to its higher-capacity siblings. It comes with a sculpted fuel tank, sporty graphics, and a twin-strip LED tail lamp that adds a touch of aggression and style.

Features

Key features include a semi-digital instrument console, halogen headlamp, electric start, and optional disc brake. It also comes with clip-on handlebars, split grab rails, and tubeless tyres for added practicality and safety.

Riding Posture & Comfort

The Pulsar 125 offers a commuter-friendly riding posture with slightly sporty ergonomics. The seat is well-cushioned for both rider and pillion, and the upright stance makes it suitable for daily rides.

Engine, Mileage & Performance

It is powered by a 124.4cc air-cooled, single-cylinder engine producing 11.8 PS and 10.8 Nm. Mated to a 5-speed gearbox, it provides peppy performance with a claimed mileage of around 50–55 kmpl.

Handling & Ride Quality

With a telescopic front fork and twin gas-charged rear shocks, the Pulsar 125 handles city roads and bumps comfortably. It is agile in traffic and stable enough for short highway runs.

Should You Buy Bajaj Pulsar 125?

If you want a sporty commuter bike with solid performance, reliable mileage, and the iconic Pulsar styling, the Pulsar 125 is a smart choice. It offers great value without compromising on the Pulsar DNA.
