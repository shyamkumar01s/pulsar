BAJAJ PLATINA CT100

Bajaj Platina 100 Summary

Price

Bajaj Platina 100 price for its variant - Platina 100 ES Drum starts at Rs. 88,606. The mentioned Platina 100 price is the on-road price of Chennai.

About

Bajaj Platina 100 is a commuter bike available in only 1 variant and 4 colours. The Bajaj Platina 100 is powered by 102cc BS6 engine which has a power of 7.79 bhp and a torque of 8.34 Nm. With both front and rear drum brakes, Bajaj Platina 100 comes up with combined braking system of both wheels. This Platina 100 bike weighs 117 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 11 liters

Bajaj Platina 100 Overview

Design

The Platina 100 has a simplistic design. It gets a spacious seat that’s padded well and the handlebar ensures an upright riding posture. Interestingly, for pillions, the bike gets flat floorboards instead of conventional footpegs, which should ideally help in pillion comfort. Among these bits, the bike’s bikini fairing and graphics add a slight sporty touch.

Features

The Platina 100 comes with a halogen headlight along with LED DRLs. Its instrument console is an analogue unit and displays bits like the speed, odometer, fuel level as well as indicator lights.

Riding Posture & Comfort

The Platina 100 is quite a comfortable bike to ride. The long and flat seat has enough room to accommodate the rider and the pillion and the handlebar is positioned well to give the rider an upright, comfortable posture.

Engine, Mileage & Performance

Powering the Platina 100 is a 102cc air-cooled engine making 7.9PS and 8.34Nm. Paired to a 4-speed gearbox, Bajaj claims that the bike can do a 90kmph top speed. Suspension duties are handled by a telescopic fork along with dual shocks. Braking, meanwhile, is taken care of by a 130mm front drum brake along with a 110mm drum unit at the rear. CBS (Combined Braking System) is standard.

Handling & Ride Quality

The Platina 100 has good handling and ride quality. It’s easy to maneuver through traffic and can handle bad roads quite easily.

Should You Buy Bajaj Platina 100?

You can buy the Platina 100 for Rs 68,685 (ex-showroom Delhi) and it’s on sale in four colour schemes. That makes it one of the most affordable 100cc bikes in India. It, hence, makes for a comfortable, minimalistic and very affordable commuter.
