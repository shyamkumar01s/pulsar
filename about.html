ABOUT US

About Us

Bajaj Auto is a global leader in two- and three-wheeler manufacturing. Established in 1945, the company has become a symbol of innovation, performance, and trust. Headquartered in Pune, India, Bajaj Auto exports to over 70 countries across Latin America, Africa, and Asia.

Our Legacy

Bajaj Auto has a rich heritage, starting from manufacturing scooters to producing cutting-edge motorcycles and commercial vehicles. The iconic Bajaj Chetak scooter, the Pulsar series, and RE auto rickshaws are benchmarks in their categories. Over the decades, Bajaj has consistently delivered quality and affordability to its customers.
