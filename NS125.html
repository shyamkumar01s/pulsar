BAJAJ PULSAR NS125

Bajaj pulsar NS 125 Summary

Price

Bajaj Pulsar NS125 price for its variant - Pulsar NS125 Standard starts at Rs. 1,22,243. The price for the other variants - Pulsar NS125 Bluetooth and Pulsar NS125 Single Channel ABS are Rs. 1,33,013 and Rs. 1,38,707. The mentioned Pulsar NS125 prices are the on-road price of Chennai.

About

Bajaj Pulsar NS125 is a commuter bike available in 3 variants and 4 colours. The Bajaj Pulsar NS125 is powered by 124.45cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 11.8 bhp and a torque of 11 Nm. With front disc and rear drum brakes, Bajaj Pulsar NS125 comes up with anti-locking braking system. This Pulsar NS125 bike weighs 144 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 12 liters.

Bajaj Pulsar NS 125 Overview

Design

The Bajaj Pulsar NS125 carries the same aggressive streetfighter design as its larger siblings. It features sharp tank extensions, a muscular fuel tank, and a sporty stance. The split grab rails and sleek tail section enhance its youthful appeal.

Features

The NS125 comes with features like a halogen headlamp, LED tail lamp, digital-analogue instrument console, and alloy wheels. It also has perimeter frame construction for better handling, a rarity in this segment.

Riding Posture & Comfort

The NS125 offers a slightly sporty yet comfortable riding posture. The seat is contoured for better grip, and the footpeg-handlebar-seat triangle provides a balanced ride position suited for both city commuting and occasional spirited riding.

Engine, Mileage & Performance

Powering the Pulsar NS125 is a 124.45cc air-cooled, single-cylinder engine producing 11.99 PS at 8500 rpm and 11 Nm at 7000 rpm. It is mated to a 5-speed gearbox. Despite its sporty nature, it delivers decent mileage of around 50–55 kmpl under normal riding conditions.

Handling & Ride Quality

The bike’s perimeter frame, telescopic front forks, and rear monoshock ensure sharp handling and confident cornering. It's agile in traffic and stable at higher speeds, offering one of the best ride dynamics in the 125cc segment.

Should You Buy Bajaj Pulsar NS125?

If you’re looking for a stylish, performance-oriented 125cc bike that’s both practical and fun to ride, the Pulsar NS125 is a solid choice. It combines sporty looks, capable handling, and reliable performance in an affordable package.
