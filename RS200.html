Bajaj pulsar RS 200 Summary

Price

Bajaj Pulsar RS 200 price for its variant - Pulsar RS 200 Standard starts at Rs. 2,10,410. The price for the other variant - Pulsar RS 200 Standard [2025] is Rs. 2,21,893. The mentioned Pulsar RS 200 prices are the on-road price of Chennai.

About

Bajaj Pulsar RS 200 is a sports bike available in 2 variants and 6 colours. The Bajaj Pulsar RS 200 is powered by 199.5cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 24.1 bhp and a torque of 18.7 Nm. With both front and rear disc brakes, Bajaj Pulsar RS 200 comes up with anti-locking braking system. This Pulsar RS 200 bike weighs 167 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 13 liters.

Bajaj Pulsar RS 200 Overview

Design

The Bajaj Pulsar RS200 features a full-faired, aggressive design inspired by racing motorcycles. It comes with sharp lines, a split LED headlamp, and aerodynamic styling that enhances both aesthetics and wind protection.

Features

It boasts an all-LED lighting setup, a fully digital instrument cluster, dual-channel ABS, slipper clutch, and a USB charging port. The RS200 also includes features like a projector headlamp and sporty graphics, elevating its premium feel.

Riding Posture & Comfort

The RS200 offers a sporty yet manageable riding posture with clip-on handlebars and a raised tail section. The split seats are well-cushioned, providing comfort for both rider and pillion during longer rides.

Engine, Mileage & Performance

Powered by a 199.5cc liquid-cooled, single-cylinder engine delivering 24.5 PS at 9750 rpm and 18.6 Nm of torque, the RS200 is paired with a 6-speed gearbox and slipper clutch. It offers brisk acceleration and a top speed close to 140 km/h, with mileage around 35–40 kmpl.

Handling & Ride Quality

Equipped with a perimeter frame, USD front forks, and a monoshock at the rear, the Pulsar RS200 delivers sharp handling and excellent stability on corners. The dual-channel ABS ensures safe braking, while the suspension balances comfort and sportiness.

Should You Buy Bajaj Pulsar RS200?

The RS200 is an ideal choice for riders seeking a sporty fully-faired motorcycle with impressive performance and modern features at a competitive price. It suits both daily commuting and weekend spirited rides.
