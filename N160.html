Bajaj Pulsar N160 Overview

Design

The Bajaj Pulsar N160 features a modern and muscular design with a distinctive LED projector headlamp, sharp tank extensions, and a sporty stance. Its aggressive styling is inspired by the Pulsar N250, giving it a premium and bold appearance for a 160cc bike.

Features

The N160 comes equipped with an LED projector headlight, LED DRLs, a digital-analogue instrument cluster, USB charging port, and dual-channel ABS (in the higher variant). It also gets sporty split grab rails, a belly pan, and stylish alloy wheels.

Riding Posture & Comfort

The bike offers a balanced riding posture that’s upright enough for daily commuting and slightly sporty for fun rides. The seat is wide and well-padded, and the handlebars are positioned to reduce fatigue over long distances, making it a practical everyday machine.

Engine, Mileage & Performance

The Pulsar N160 is powered by a 164.82cc oil-cooled single-cylinder engine producing 16 PS at 8750 rpm and 14.65 Nm at 6750 rpm. It’s paired with a 5-speed gearbox and delivers smooth and linear performance. It offers a mileage of around 45–50 kmpl, making it efficient for its segment.

Handling & Ride Quality

Built on a robust frame and equipped with a telescopic fork and a rear monoshock, the N160 offers precise handling and a planted feel. Whether riding through city traffic or on twisty roads, it feels composed and nimble. The dual-channel ABS adds confidence during hard braking.

Should You Buy Bajaj Pulsar N160?

If you're looking for a stylish, feature-loaded, and well-balanced 160cc motorcycle, the Bajaj Pulsar N160 is an excellent choice. It delivers on performance, safety, and comfort while remaining affordable and suitable for both daily use and weekend rides.
